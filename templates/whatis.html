What is ?

YouNotify allows subscribers to get sms notification when a new video is uploaded to their subscribed channels. In fact, YouTube users must always search if a new video is uploaded or wait for the weekly notifications. When a video has just been added and therefore does not have many views, this video does not appear in the first results. So you can think that there is no new video. With YouNotify, you will be alerted by sms and by mail as soon as a video is added to the chosen channels. YouNotify helps by the same time channels owner to have more subscribers because. Indeed, Youtube users do not see the need to subscribe to channels because subscribed or not, you have to search videos you want and watch it. YouNotify solves this problem because users are obliged to subscribe to channels if they want to get our notifications.
